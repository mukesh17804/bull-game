
Bull Embrace – The One with Hugs (PWA)
======================================

A small, original arcade game with a Friends-inspired cozy vibe. Move your circle to the friendly bull and press HUG at the safe moments (calm or tired). Avoid hugging during a charge!

Inside:
- index.html, style.css, game.js
- manifest.json, service-worker.js
- bg_loop.wav (royalty-free loop)
- icon-*.png (72–512)

Run:
1) Unzip.
2) Serve locally: `python -m http.server 8080` then open http://localhost:8080
3) Click "Play Music" to start audio (autoplay blocked by browsers).
4) Install from browser menu for full-screen PWA.

This is a fan homage with no official logos or copyrighted audio.
