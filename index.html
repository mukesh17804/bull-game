How to play

  • Move your circle to gently embrace the friendly bull during calm or tired moments.
  • Avoid hugging while it's charging — you'll lose a heart!
  • Mouse/Touch to move. Hold Space or press the on-screen "Hug" to embrace.
  • Chain safe hugs for combo points.
